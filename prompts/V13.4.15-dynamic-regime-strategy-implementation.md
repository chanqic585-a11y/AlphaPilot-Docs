# 9. Regime Router

输出：

```text
trend
mean_reversion
avoid
```

Trend 条件草案：

```text
4h close > EMA200
4h EMA20 > EMA50
1h close > EMA50
BTC safe
```

Mean reversion 条件草案：

```text
4h not strong downtrend
BTC not crash
price below or near lower Bollinger
RSI low or recovering
volatility acceptable
```

Avoid 条件：

```text
BTC crash
data missing
liquidity unavailable
extreme ATR
strong 4h downtrend
```
